# Monopoly - Game Description

**Monopoly** is a classic board game centered around real estate trading and financial strategy. The objective is to become the wealthiest player by buying, renting, and selling properties.

## Key Features

- **Players:** 2â€“8
- **Goal:** Become the richest player and bankrupt your opponents.

## How to Play

- Players take turns rolling two dice to move around the board.
- The board consists of:
  - **Properties** (grouped by color)
  - **Railroads and utilities**
  - **Tax spaces**
  - **Chance and Community Chest cards**
  - **Special spaces** like *Jail*, *Free Parking*, and *Go to Jail*
- If you land on an unowned property, you can **buy** it from the bank.
- If you land on a property owned by another player, you must **pay rent**.
- Owning all properties of a color group allows you to **build houses and hotels**, increasing the rent.

## Strategy

- Acquire **monopolies** to build and charge higher rent.
- **Negotiate trades** with other players to gain advantages.
- **Manage your money** carefully to avoid bankruptcy
